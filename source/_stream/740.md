Today’s findings:

1. I really need to practice unclipping from the pedals when I hit obstacles (especially my left foot)

2. The <a href="https://www.instagram.com/goproanz">@goproanz</a> Chesty mount is awesome, but I need to adjust the angle of the camera.

3. I need to pick a new helmet - more specifically one with a visor. I might as well wear a blindfold when climbing up into the early morning sun.

Was a little more cautious out in the trails than usual - mainly because it was my first time in a long time clipping in while off-road - but also things were pretty sketchy after the rain earlier this week.  All-in-all though, a morning of good clean fun (and by ‘clean’ I mean muddy af!).
