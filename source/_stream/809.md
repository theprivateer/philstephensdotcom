Taking some baseline measurements for the start of the year. 

Whilst I probably won’t be back on the bike for another week or so while my body heals up I don’t want to use that as an excuse to not start pursuing some semblance of a healthier lifestyle.  Whilst I am by no means obese, I have pretty respectable dad-bod happening and could definitely benefit from a bit of weight loss (even if it just means having to carry less up the hill when riding the bike).

Haven’t really got much of a plan at the moment, other than riding the bikes more, I’m hoping that by laying it all out there for all to see it will make me a bit more accountable when I get started in earnest.

Whilst I doubt I’ll be jumping on the scales every day, I’ll be using my old <a href="https://www.instagram.com/withings">@withings</a> scale to keep track of my overall body composition throughout the year.  So here it is, the Week One weigh-in:

Weight: 90.1kg
Fat: 22.7%
Muscle: 66.3kg
BMI: 27.5
(for reference I am 181cm tall)

Will post a monthly update and hopefully start seeing some of those figures changing in the right direction.
