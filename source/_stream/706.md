So in the spirit of making 2019 (and beyond) all about cycling, I may have just entered my first ever mountain bike race 😳 . Managed to get in early and nab one of the $20 entries to the <a href="https://www.instagram.com/in_2_adventure">@in_2_adventure</a> Sunshine Coast MTB festival before they sold out.

I now have until the end of April to train for a 50km XC race around the Sunshine Coast Hinterland - hopefully this will be the first of many! (to be honest I just want to make sure I finish!) .
