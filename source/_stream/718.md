Took the new stand-up paddle board out for a spin. Did not stand up.

Water was looking particularly good this morning when I was out on my ride so came back down with the family for a little beach time. Water was getting a bit choppy for a novice like me to keep their balance so opted to kneel/kayak on the board instead. Still great fun though.
