Today we promoted my eldest to 20” wheels and a proper ‘big girl bike’ - picked up a <a href="https://www.instagram.com/trekbikesau">@trekbikesau</a> Precaliber 20 to match my X-Caliber from the good folk down at <a href="https://www.instagram.com/trekbicyclewoolloongabba">@trekbicyclewoolloongabba</a> (had to pick out a new <a href="https://www.instagram.com/ridebontrager">@ridebontrager</a> helmet and gloves as well to mark the occasion).

It’s a touch on the large side for her at the moment but she’ll grow into it in no time - and besides, after about 5 minutes of getting the measure of it she smashed out 3km as if it was nothing. Looking forward to taking this one out on the cycleway with me as her confidence (and legs!) grows.
