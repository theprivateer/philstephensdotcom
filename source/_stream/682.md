After bordering on six years of active service, it’s time to retire my first ever set of road cycling shoes. Picked up these <a href="https://www.instagram.com/maviccycling">@maviccycling</a> shoes from an independent bike shop back in Edinburgh (can’t remember the name) - had just bought my first road bike, complete with a set of SPD-SL pedals and didn’t have a clue (tbh I mainly liked the yellow flashes on these!). I totally lucked out with my choice. They have served me well over the years but there’s only so many times you can glue the sole back on!
