Woke up bright and early for the <a href="https://www.instagram.com/pedalheadscycles">@pedalheadscycles</a> shop ride. Feeling a little tight in the chest, so with my pericarditis flaring up last week post-Velothon I decided to go for a gentler solo ride out to Scarborough where I could focus on keeping my heart rate in a lower range, rather than keeping pace with the squad. 
Was rewarded with a beautiful crisp morning and fantastic views our over a still Moreton Bay. 
I even got to join the PedalHeads Inter 2 group for about 10 seconds as they passed me in Scarborough 😜
