Century Sunday 💯


Banked my first 100+ km ride of 2019 this morning - actually my first since the Noosa Classic last August.

.
Bit of a last minute decision - wanted to see how I’d fare over a longer distance with the last two months of training under my belt. Really happy with my performance - especially over the last quarter. Even though my legs were definitely feeling fatigued I somehow managed to ride faster and stronger than ever 🤷🏻‍♂️

.
My only regret - finishing my ride at about 9am, it’s far too early to crack open a cold beer (it is, right?). Had to make do with a coffee instead.

.
Looking forward to getting more of these in on a regular basis - next milestone will be the Imperial Century - 100 miles (160km).
