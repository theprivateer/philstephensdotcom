Tastebuds are still buzzing after last nights Chef's Tasting Meal at <a href="https://www.instagram.com/cucaflavor">@cucaflavor</a> - nine exquisitely different and delicious tasting dishes, accompanied by fantastic cocktails. Pictures simply do not do the craftsmanship of each dish justice. Whilst it's hard to pick a favourite, the BBQ Octopus is unforgettable - anyone that's ever tried it will know what I mean when I say the flavour and texture is mind blowing! Kudos to <a href="https://www.instagram.com/tamsyno">@tamsyno</a> for researching and finding the place, but I fear we may have peaked too early with our dining on this holiday!

16/10 - would dine again!
