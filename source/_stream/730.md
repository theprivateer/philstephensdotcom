Through a combination of the Instagram algorithm and Gmail’s spam settings I missed the fact that it was the <a href="https://www.instagram.com/cycleofgiving">@cycleofgiving</a> today, so decided to do another self-directed 💯km ride this morning.

.
Four laps of the <a href="https://www.instagram.com/translinkqld">@translinkqld</a> Redcliffe Peninsula Line cycleway - a great rolling surface and more than a few hills means it was far from monotonous. Definitely adding this as a regular fixture to my riding. Has also given me an idea for an additional challenge for 2019 - more to follow once I’ve thought about the logistics a little more.
