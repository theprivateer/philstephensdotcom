Starting the day right!  It's been at least 5-6 years since I last rode Jurassic (2) and Carnivore (6) at Bunya - both black diamond trails.  Partly because of the scary names 😜, but mainly due to lack of fitness.
.
The fitness still isn't there, but today I put on my big boy pants and gave them both a go.  Will need a couple more practice runs before I do either of them at speed, but managed to get through them both without any spills.
.
Also my first time riding up the rock garden out of Creek Track (4) - small victories 💪🏻.
