Dust, mud, lizards, gravel and yoga.

Today I started a five week training block leading up to the <a href="https://www.instagram.com/ontheedgeevents">@ontheedgeevents</a> Kooralbyn MTB Endurance Series. Kicked things off with a two hour XC slog round the 7km training loop I’ve scoped out at the trails up at <a href="https://www.instagram.com/northharbourliving">@northharbourliving</a>.

Faired better than expected, but still a heap of work to be done over the coming weeks.

Added an extra couple hundred metres onto each lap to avoid the Sunday morning yoga in the heritage park - can’t imagine my sweaty, puffing form whizzing past every twenty minutes would have helped anyone’s Downward Facing Dog 😳
