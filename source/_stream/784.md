At the beginning of October I decided to go alcohol-free for at least a month - no mean feat when you are a self-professed beer enthusiast. I did this previously in August, and it felt great, but I was finding that my options at the local bottleshops were sometimes a little limited if you wanted something other than a non-alcoholic lager (hoppy ales is more my jam). I was stoked to discover <a href="https://www.instagram.com/craftzeroau">@craftzeroau</a> and have just received my first delivery of a selection of beers from around the globe.
