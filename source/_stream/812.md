We’ve been pretty lucky in Queensland up until now with COVID, largely avoiding the lockdowns and restrictions that have affected NSW and Victoria, but a Brisbane lockdown was inevitable once they started to detect signs of community transmission again.

Hopefully this is more of a preemptive lockdown to nip the latest outbreak in the bud before it becomes a longer-term thing.

Stay safe everyone, be sensible and look after each other.
