Stoked to have found these <a href="https://www.instagram.com/tastinz">@tastinz</a> Smooshed Wholefood Balls at my local <a href="https://www.instagram.com/aldiaustralia">@aldiaustralia</a> store. For the past few weeks they have been my go to for nutrition whilst out on the bike. Delicious, compact, clean energy - I find I digest them much better than any energy gels. The peanut butter and caramel are my current jam.
