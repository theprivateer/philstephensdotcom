Tough but enjoyable Great Brisbane Bike Ride yesterday.  Hit up the 110km course - minus the optional climb up Mt Coot-tha because, damn, there were plenty of hills without it!  Time was slightly longer than expected, but given the 1,400m of climbing I’m not too disappointed.

Hat’s off to <a href="https://www.instagram.com/bicycle_qld">@bicycle_qld</a> for another great event - organisation was slick as ever.  Great to see they had wrangled exclusive use of the Moggill Ferry, avoiding the hour and a half wait from two years ago.

10/10 would ride again
