Couldn’t resist getting one last ride in for 2018!

I was going to give it another couple weeks of relaxed road riding before hitting the trails, but after getting out on the mountain bike with kids this morning I was really hankering for a bit of dusty action!

First time riding both a hard-tail AND a 29er at Bunyaville, and man, that sure was a slice of fried fun! The <a href="https://www.instagram.com/trekbikes">@trekbikes</a> X-Caliber 9 delivered the goods - great handling and climbed almost as fast as it descended! What a great way to round out the year.
