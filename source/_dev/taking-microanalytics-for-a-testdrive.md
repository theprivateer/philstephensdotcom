Ever since I relaunched this website I've wanted a simple way to track usage (as little as it may be).  I don't have any sort of comment functionality or newsletter to track engagement, and I am loathe to install Google Analytics.

I was going to try the simple, privacy-first offering from [Fathom Analytics](https://usefathom.com) because, well, they're not Google (and an active part of the Laravel community) but have been a little hesitant due to the lack of a free tier for minuscule sites like my own.  Don't get me wrong, I have no problem paying for indie services like this - in fact I actively subscribe to other small services - but I didn't feel that the 7-day free trial was enough to capture the low traffic I expect my site to be receiving.

In steps Microana
